globalMines

land cover

Global, spatial distribution of mining operations

Details

This dataset draws individual mining operations, derived through a dense database of Landsat acquisitions. Individual polygons are drawn within a 10 km buffer from the approximate geographical coordinates of more than 6,000 active mining sites across the globe.

Subdatasets
